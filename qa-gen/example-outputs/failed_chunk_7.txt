**Q1: What insurance rating and admission requirements must the Contractor's insurance company meet?**  
**A1:** The insurance must be issued by a company admitted within the Commonwealth of Virginia and with at least a Best’s Key Rating of A:V1.  
**Text Snippet:** "Such insurance must be issued by a company admitted within the Commonwealth of Virginia and with at least a Best’s Key Rating of A:V1."  

**Q2: What actions must the Contractor take if goods are lost or damaged in transit?**  
**A2:** The Contractor must make immediate replacement of the damaged or lost merchandise or be in default of the contract. The Contractor is also responsible for filing a claim against the carrier.  
**Text Snippet:** "The contractor shall make immediate replacement of the damaged or lost merchandise or be in default of the contract. It shall be the contractor’s responsibility to file a claim against the carrier."
